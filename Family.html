Naruto Uzumaki' Family


Naruto Uzumaki comes from a remarkable lineage, starting with his parents, Minato Namikaze, the Fourth Hokage known for his incredible speed and strength, and Kushina Uzumaki, a strong and loving kunoichi with a fiery spirit. Naruto grew up without his parents, who died protecting the village shortly after his birth. As he grew older, Naruto married Hinata Hyuga, a kind and gentle ninja from the noble Hyuga clan, known for her Byakugan and unwavering love for Naruto. Together, they have two children: Boruto, a talented and sometimes rebellious young ninja seeking his own path, and Himawari, their cheerful and sweet daughter who also shows great potential.
