Naruto Uzumaki' Great Feats

1. Befriending Kurama (Nine-Tails)

  • At first, Kurama, the Nine-Tails sealed inside Naruto, was a source of

    hatred and destruction — constantly battling Naruto from within. But

    instead of suppressing or fearing the beast, Naruto chose to understand

    his pain, showing him empathy and trust. Over time, their bond deepened,

    and Kurama willingly shared his immense chakra with Naruto, making them

    one of the most powerful duos in history. This feat wasn't just about

    power — it was about healing centuries of hatred between humans and tailed

    beasts

2: Defeating Pain (Nagato)

  • When the village was devastated by Pain's attack, Naruto arrived as the lone

    warrior standing against him. Using his Sage Mode, intelligence, and sheer

    determination, he defeated all Six Paths of Pain. But his greatest act wasn't

    victory in battle — it was choosing peace. Instead of killing Nagato, Naruto

    listened to his story, shared Jiraiya’s teachings, and inspired him to believe

    in hope again. Through compassion, he turned an enemy into a believer, saving

    not just the village, but a soul lost in pain.

3. The War Hero – Fourth Great Ninja War

  • During the Fourth Great Ninja War, Naruto rose as the heart of the Allied

    Shinobi Forces. He shared his chakra across the battlefield, empowered

    thousands, and fought alongside legends to stop world-ending threats like

    Obito, Madara, and Kaguya Ōtsutsuki. His unwavering spirit, compassion, and

    sheer power unified nations and saved the shinobi world. More than a warrior,

    Naruto became the symbol of hope and resilience in humanity’s darkest hour.

4. Becoming seventh Hokage

  • For Naruto, becoming Hokage wasn’t just a title — it was the embodiment of

    everything he had fought for. From a lonely boy shunned by the village, he worked

    tirelessly to earn their love and respect. Through countless battles, unwavering

    compassion, and unbreakable will, he proved himself not just as a powerful ninja,

    but as a true leader. When he finally stood as the Seventh Hokage, it wasn’t just

    a personal victory — it was the fulfillment of a lifelong dream built on pain,

    perseverance, and the desire to protect everyone he cared about.

5. Mastering Sage of Six Paths Powers

  • Naruto gains the Sage of Six Paths powers during the Fourth Great Ninja War after

    receiving chakra from Hagoromo Ōtsutsuki. This grants him abilities like flight,

    enhanced healing, sensory perception, Truth-Seeking Orbs, and mastery over all

    chakra natures. He uses these powers to battle Madara, Kaguya, and later, Sasuke.

    In Boruto, Naruto still retains these abilities and uses them during major

    fights, including his intense battle against Momoshiki Ōtsutsuki, where he teams

    up with Sasuke to protect the village.

6. Naruto the strongest of Mankind - Baryone Mode

  • Baryon Mode is Naruto’s ultimate transformation, introduced during his battle with

    Isshiki Ōtsutsuki. Unlike previous forms that relied on stored chakra, Baryon Mode

    works by fusing Naruto’s and Kurama’s chakra at a fundamental level — similar to

    nuclear fusion — burning their life force to generate a surge of overwhelming

    power. This form granted Naruto speed and strength far beyond his Sage of Six

    Paths mode, with reflexes so sharp even Isshiki struggled to keep up. Every attack

    Naruto landed in this state drained his opponent’s lifespan, making even a single

    hit deadly. However, the power came at a steep cost — the fusion consumed Kurama’s

    life, leading to his death and the permanent loss of the form. Baryon Mode wasn’t

    just powerful; it was a last-resort, god-slaying force born out of desperation and

    love for his village.
